Association model & sources

Geometry: population tractography contours from HCP-1065. They are not individual axons and do not contain measured polarity.

Direction: sampled per event using an explicit 50/50 source-to-endpoint assumption for every named association tract. Diffusion MRI cannot distinguish afferent from efferent axons, and this review found no defensible quantitative human ratios.

Timing: rates, per-channel refractory self-inhibition and recovery, and travel speed are illustrative display algorithms—not measured spikes, conduction speed, or neuronal physiology.

Polarity limits · ILF · IFOF · SLF · VOF · Arcuate · MdLF

Each hemisphere sees the opposite half-field: nasal-retinal fibres cross at the chiasm, the LGN relays, then the optic radiation carries the signal back to V1 — the upper field detouring forward through Meyer's loop.

Real data (MNI152): cortical surface, the amber optic radiation (HCP-1065 streamlines — real fibre density), and the LGN, V1, V2, hOc5 region shells (Jülich-Brain maximum-probability map — consistent cytoarchitectonic boundaries).
